Landsat Reflectance Project Overview

Our project focused on tracking the real-time position of Landsat satellites including Landsat 2, 3, 7, and 8. Using satellite data APIs, the system calculates the time, distance required for a satellite to pass over a user-defined location.

  • Real-time satellite tracking for Landsat 2, 3, 7, and 8
  • User-defined location and range adjustment
  • Email notifications via EmailJS when satellite is in range
  • Comparison of satellite images and ground survey photos (Under Progress)
  • Automation of agricultural and geographical data collection (Under Progress)

    • The core aim of the project was to automate the manual inspection of geographical and agricultural areas using remote sensing and satellite data. By analyzing and comparing captured satellite images with ground-level survey photos, users can monitor changes in land use, vegetation health, and geographical patterns without physically visiting the area.

    Our project was recognized under the NASA Space Apps 2023 Challenge and affiliated globally with agencies like NASA, ISRO, EESA, AEM, CSA, and more.
